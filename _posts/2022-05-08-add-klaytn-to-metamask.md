### 3. 아래 Klaytn Network 정보를 입력 후 "저장" 클릭
* Network Name: Klaytn Mainnet
* New RPC URL: https://public-node-api.klaytnapi.com/v1/cypress
* Chain ID: 8217
* Current Symbol: KLAY
* Block Explorer URL: https://scope.klaytn.com
